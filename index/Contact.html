Meet The Team!

Diana Capellan

Team Lead

Diana Capellan is the leader of the Pill Pal development team, responsible for coordinating the project from start to finish. She is an experienced software developer and project coordinator with a passion for improving healthcare outcomes through technology. With Diana's guidance, the Pill Pal team created an app that provides users with easy access to information about their medications, helping them make informed decisions about their health.

Jorge Medina

Back-End Engineer

Jorge Medina is an accomplished back-end engineer who made significant contributions to the development of the Pill Pal app. His primary responsibility was creating an API that gathers user data from the app and stores it in our database. This API plays a critical role in the functionality of the app, allowing users to easily access their medication history and providing valuable insights into their health. In addition to his work on the API, Jorge also provided additional back-end support to other engineers on the team.

Dante Hopkins

Front-End Engineer

Dante Hopkins is a skilled front-end engineer who made important contributions to the development of the Pill Pal app. His primary responsibility was designing and implementing the contact page, which allows users to easily get in touch with the Pill Pal team. Dante also played a key role in writing the descriptions for the Pill Pal team members.

Andres Barreras

Back-End Engineer

Andres Barreras is a skilled software developer who played a critical role in the development of the Pill Pal app. His primary contribution was integrating the OpenFDA API, which provides the app with accurate and up-to-date medication information. In addition, Andres contributed to the front-end development of the app, particularly on the home page.

Sincere Legere

Front-End Engineer

Sincere Legere is a talented front-end developer who played a key role in the development of the Pill Pal app. His primary responsibility was designing and implementing the home page of the app. With his expertise in user interface design, Sincere created an engaging and visually appealing user experience that welcomes users and provides easy access to the app's key features.
