PillPal

What we are all about

PillPal is a powerful drug interaction checker app that enables users to quickly and easily check potential drug interactions and conflicts between different medications. This open-source project provides a fast and reliable solution to help individuals and healthcare professionals to identify and prevent harmful drug interactions. The PillPal app comes with a comprehensive database of drugs and their potential interactions, which users can search and browse to determine if any of their prescribed or over-the-counter medications may interact with each other and cause adverse effects.
